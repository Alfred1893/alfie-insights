## The Four Boundaries of AI Automation

**What it is:** A task triage framework that classifies any "let AI do X for me" request into one of four structural categories.

**The core insight:** The question "can AI do X for me?" is the wrong question. The right question is "which of the four categories does X fall into, and what is the non-delegable responsibility for that category?"

**The four categories:**

| # | Task Type | AI Autonomy | Non-delegable Responsibility |
|---|-----------|-------------|------------------------------|
| 1 | User-Initiated Actions | 0% | Your value proposition itself |
| 2 | Closed-Loop Operations | 90-100% | Audit rights |
| 3 | Judgment Calls | 70-80% prep | Final decision + accountability |
| 4 | Relationship Building | 60-70% prep | The final human-to-human moment |

## Anthropic's Hidden AGI Roadmap (May 21, 2026 source leak analysis)

**What it is:** A hot-take analysis of what the May 21, 2026 Claude Code source code leak actually revealed — not 5 random features, but 5 components of one product definition: a digital employee.

**The synthesis:** Anthropic is not competing on model strength. They are competing on organizational capability. The 5 hidden features (KAIROS, Nightly Dreaming, ULTRAPLAN, Coordinator Mode, BUDDY) map directly to the 4 core capabilities of a human employee: memory, digestion, planning, collaboration, plus companionship.
